Why use a job tracker?

I applied here?

During my job search process, I soon learned that this is a numbers game. Meaning many applications will have to go out for very little responces. How do you keep track of all of them? I've recieved calls and my first thought was, 'I applied here??'. That cant happen!

Am I applying to enough job?

When it comes to a job hunt anyone can tell you that it is a full time job in itself! Somedays you feel like you were applying all day and feel successful, but in reality the number of jobs may have only been 2 or 3. This will help you keep track of application quotas!

Follow Up?

Has a company ghosted you ? Or told you they would contact you in a certain time frame but didnt? This app allows you to add follow up notes, plus we keep track of the day you applied!

Responces

This app will allow you to check off the companies decision on your application so you dont forget within the choas.
